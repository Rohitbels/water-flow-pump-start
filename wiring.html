05 — Assembly Steps
1
POWER OFF EVERYTHING. Turn off mains MCB. Confirm with multimeter no voltage on the line. Do this before touching any wires.
2
Plumbing first. Install the YF-B3 sensor inline on the inlet pipe with brass unions on both sides. Arrow on sensor body must point toward the pump. Use Teflon tape on threads. Install the mesh strainer just before the sensor.
3
Wire the DC side. Connect NodeMCU to relay and sensor using jumper wires per the connection table above. All connections inside the enclosure.
Key pins: D1 → Relay IN, D2 → YF-B3 yellow, 5V → Relay VCC + YF-B3 red, GND → Relay GND + YF-B3 black
4
Wire the AC side. Use 2.5mm² wire only. Connect mains L to Relay COM. Connect Relay NO to Pump L. Connect mains N directly to Pump N. Connect mains Earth to Pump body.
5
Verify the relay jumper. The 30A module has a HIGH/LOW trigger jumper. Set it to HIGH so the relay activates when D1 goes HIGH (matches the code).
6
Mount everything in the IP65 enclosure. NodeMCU + relay + USB adapter inside. Use cable glands for wires entering/exiting. Pump should be outside the box.
7
Flash the Arduino code (will be provided next). Connect NodeMCU to your laptop via USB, upload the sketch.
8
First power-on test. Turn on MCB. NodeMCU should boot, create WiFi "GardenPump" hotspot. Connect phone, open http://192.168.4.1. Use manual ON button to test pump. Listen for relay click + pump start.
06 — Safety Critical DO NOT SKIP
⚠ EARTH IS MANDATORY. The pump motor body MUST be earthed. Without it, any internal short turns the metal body live at 230V. Anyone touching it on wet ground = serious shock. Run a green wire from mains earth to the pump's earth terminal (marked with ⏚ symbol).
⚠ NEVER WORK ON LIVE CIRCUITS. Always switch off the 6A MCB before opening the enclosure or touching any wire. Verify with a multimeter or non-contact voltage tester.
⚠ KEEP DC AND AC SEPARATE. Inside the enclosure, keep low-voltage wires (NodeMCU side, sensor side) physically separated from 230V wires. Use the divider/partition if your box has one. Cable ties to bundle DC wires together away from AC.
⚠ WATERPROOF THE ENCLOSURE. IP65 rated box only. Cable glands on every wire entry. Mount with cable glands facing DOWN so any water drips away. Silicone sealant around any imperfect seals.
⚠ INSTALL A 6A MCB upstream. Don't skip this. If anything shorts, the MCB trips and saves your wiring/equipment from burning.
